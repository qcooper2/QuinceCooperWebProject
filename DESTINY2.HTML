Lets get into Destiny 2

Welcome guardian! lookin to jump into destiny 2 and get started with a class. A class offers unique abilitys dependendant on which of the three is picked. The classes in destiny consist of a hunter, titan and warlock. Each class offers multiple ways to play the game in a fresh way with five subclasses being Arc, Solar, Void Stasis and Strand. The three classes have access to these abilities but they will offer different abilities based on the classes picked.

Get Started

Hunter

Hunters are known for their agile and stealthy traits.The abilities iclude throwing knives, Smoke bombs a A lighting surge and Rolling dodge.

Warlock

Warlocks are known for their powerful magic and sorcery-like abilities. The ablilities include healing/empowering rifts, void projectiles and lighting surges.

Titan

The resilient Titans are the front-line defenders and brawlers. Their abilities include throwable solar hammers, void shields and arc slams.

Events

Stay up-to-date with the latest Destiny 2 patches, expansions, and more. Be on the lookout for Iron banner, a pvp event lasting 2 weeks that can give great weapons for veteran and beginner players. The Final Shape, is just around the corner!
